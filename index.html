Climate Crisis in Nigeria

Drought Issue

Nigeria is a Country in Africa that deals with many forms of droughts that create a scarce source of food for its 218.5 million residents.

Flooding is causing trouble with growing crops and for traveling

Flooding is a masive issue along with droughts in Nigeria. The effects of Climate change has created rise of sea levels in certain areas creating an enviroment not able to grow crops for its mass population.

Deforestation

Deforestation is a term where it is purposeful to remove forest land for agriculture or for manufacturing. This is very harmful over all but especially with Nigeria because they use it to burn fossil fuels which creates even more problems for their food and water scarce.

Solutions:

  • Eliminate fossil fuels.

  • Invest in safer energy sources.

  • Invest in erosion and watershed management infrastructure to reduce land degradation.
